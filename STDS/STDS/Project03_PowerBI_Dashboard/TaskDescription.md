## Task Objective
Design and develop a professional interactive Power BI dashboard to enable
management teams to track and analyze sales performance from 2022 to 2024
and support data-driven decision making.

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard consists of four main pages providing a comprehensive analysis
of sales, customers, products, and marketing performance.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Summary
Provides a high-level overview of key performance indicators including:
- Number of customers
- Closed deals
- Revenue
- Profit
- Overall rating score

### 2. Customer Analysis
Detailed analysis of customer distribution by:
- Subscription plan (Basic / Premium)
- Sales representatives
- Geographic regions
- Sales channels
- Marketing campaigns  
Includes an interactive table displaying detailed customer-level data.

### 3. Sales & Product Analysis
Analyzes:
- Sales performance of products and services
- Conversion rates
- Number of orders
- Profitability by product and sales team

### 4. Marketing & Conversion Analysis
Evaluates:
- Marketing campaign effectiveness
- Customer acquisition sources
- Conversion rate by marketing channel
- Impact of each channel on revenue

## Key Features
- Professional and interactive dashboard design
- Dynamic filters for plan, team, region, source, and time period
- Accurate and actionable insights to support fast decision-making

- Power BI
